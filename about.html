Our Story


The founding idea was that the pupils should not fall behind bad School system which overseas. Over time the School slowly grew to a Kindergarten, then standard one and by the turn of the years, a full Primary School was established. From this point, the School has catered for pupils from Prep one to standard seven.

Who we are

We are an accredited school providing an outstanding Tanzanian education. Academic achievement is at the heart of the school.

Your child will find an atmosphere of purposeful curiosity, essential for developing the love of learning that will propel them forward in the world they will enter

In tandem with an academic focus, our school makes every effort to encourage every child to develop in other important ways to` life skills such as confidence, good manners, public speaking and conversational ability. We also introduce our children, whatever their talents, to the joys and opportunities outside the classroom in the arts, sports, hobbies, teamwork and leadership activities.
